About Me.

     I was born and raised in Baltimore, Maryland and currently reside in Columbia, South Carolina.
     Most recently, I've been serving as the Director of Music & Liturgy at historic Saint Peter’s Catholic Church in Columbia since 2006. I have previously been on the faculty rosters of the University of Miami and Florida International University while also serving as coach, accompanist, and tenor in the professional chorus of Florida Grand Opera. I've appeared as visiting vocal coach, tenor soloist, and accompanist with the opera and choral departments at the University of South Carolina School of Music, including during their Concert Choir’s 2008 Olympic Tour of China.

     Internationally, I've been blessed with opportunities to perform in Canada, England, Wales, Italy, Belgium, the Netherlands, Sweden, Finland, Estonia, and the Czech Republic, as well as being the first American organist to perform in Southwest Russia under the sponsorship of the State Philharmonia on Caucasus Mineral Waters.
     I've been featured as ensemble soloist with the Grammy-winning Atlanta Symphony Orchestra Chorus and Chamber Chorus, been broadcast on CBS This Morning, PBS, Public Radio International, and BBC Radio2, and been one of the few organists ever reviewed in Opera News.
