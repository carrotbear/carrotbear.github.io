Biography


Raised in rural Harford County, Maryland and trained at Westminster Choir College, Mark Husey settled in Los Angeles in 2016 and is acclaimed as a “world renowned organist” and “YouTube influencer.”

He has concertized in Belgium, China, Estonia, Finland, Italy, Russia, Sweden, and the United Kingdom; his Saint Jude Fanfare has been performed at the Vatican. He’s been featured with the GRAMMY-winning Atlanta Symphony Orchestra Chorus, Florida Grand Opera, Symphony Orchestra Augusta, the Tropical Baroque Festival, Red Priest, New Trinity Baroque, and the Opera Camerata of Washington. His ten years as Director of Music and Liturgy at historic Saint Peter’s Catholic Church generated his first two YouTube channels MarkHusey and VisitStPeters and inspired the first Peragallo pipe organs in Arizona and Texas.

Husey has served at Church of the Blessed Sacrament (Hollywood) and Holy Trinity Catholic Church (San Pedro) and has assisted at the Cathedral of Our Lady of the Angels, and Christ Cathedral (Garden Grove). Recent performances as organist include Handel’s Messiah with the Dream Orchestra, Fauré’s Requiem with the Idyllwild Festival Orchestra and Chorus at Walt Disney Concert Hall, and as organ soloist with the award-winning Gracias Choir on the monumental organ at Los Angeles’ First Congregational Church
