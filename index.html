Mark Husey

Organist, Conductor, Accompanist, & Tenor

Biography

     Award-winning and critically-acclaimed American organist Mark Husey [hju-zi] is project liaison with the Peragallo Organ Company and the Cathedral of Saints Simon and Jude for their first pipe organ: Peragallo’s first installation in the Southwest. He recently completed 10 years as Organist and Choirmaster at historic Saint Peter's Catholic Church where his YouTube channels of over 700 videos of sacred choral and organ music garnered over 3 million views combined.
     He has appeared as visiting vocal coach, tenor soloist, and accompanist with the opera and choral departments at the University of South Carolina School of Music, most notably in their Concert Choir's 2008 Olympic Tour of China, performing in Beijing, Xi'an, and Shanghai. He

previously served on the faculty rosters of the University of Miami and Florida International University, and was accompanist and tenor with Florida Grand Opera's Education Outreach and Chorus.
     With additional performances in Canada, England, Wales, Italy, Belgium, the Netherlands, Sweden, Finland, Estonia, and the Czech Republic, he was the first American-born organist and choral clinician to perform and lead workshops in Southwest Russia under the sponsorship of the State Philharmonia on Caucasus Mineral Waters.
     He and his partner/webmaster Christopher Link reside in Los Angeles.
